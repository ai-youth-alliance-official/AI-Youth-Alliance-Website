Different uses of AI

Here is the question, where AI can be used? AI can be used in nearly every field, including healthcare, entertainment, and self-driving cars.

● Healthcare: AI can predict and diagnose disease faster than most medical professionals. Due to the development in technology, AI is speeding the process of designing the drugs and predicting any side effects.

● Entertainment: Social media services such as Instagram and YouTube use AI to recommend videos and content based on the user's interest.

● Self-driving cars: AI plays a key role in autonomous vehicles, analyzing road conditions and making decisions to ensure safety.

Right now, there are a lot of young minds that are developing AI solutions and apps. For example, there are some high school and college students that are making AI-tools that can help to monitor health, for example, tracking the beating of the heart, counting the steps you made for a day.

● Art:: DALL-E and DeepArt, AI tools which allow creators to generate art by combining new filters and tools in order to create a masterpiece.

● Privacy concerns: There are a lot of AI-powered tools and systems that can compose a song by using existing genres of music. Songwriters are already using it to make the content in seconds.

● Movies: Directors can create a plot in seconds by using AI. And nowadays, few people spend a lot of money on creating visual effects because AI creates and develops it all.

Young people are also using AI in creative ways. For example, high school students experiment with AI tools to design unusual digital art, compose music, and even direct short films
